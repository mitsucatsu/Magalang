Nauvoo Farm Resort

Nauvoo Farm Resort is nestled at the foot of Mt Arayat , Brgy Ayala, Magalang, Pampanga. It provides easy access to Banal Na Bundok, a life size Station of the Cross which is popular religious destination during lenten season. Our guests can choose from a variety of accommodation choices, ranging from cozy studio bedroom to spacious 14 pax capacity Villas. Onsite amenities include a function hall, swimming pool, bar, kitchen. Our facilities are open for venue hosting to celebrate special occasion. Come breath the fresh air and experience the beauty of Mt Arayat.

Fiona's Farm

Abe's Farm

Abe's Farm is designed to evoke a traditional Filipino rural setting, with its native-style huts, lush gardens, and relaxing ambiance. The resort aims to showcase the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines, offering a menu that features a variety of traditional Filipino dishes with a modern twist. The menu often includes regional specialties and dishes that reflect the diversity of Filipino cuisine.

Altezza Cabins

Escape the hustle and bustle and immerse yourself in nature's embrace at our serene Nature Cabins. Unwind, rejuvenate, and create cherished memories amidst breathtaking landscapes. Discover serenity nestled among the mountains. Our charming cabins offer an enchanting escape.

La Serene Private Resort

The resort's architecture is a masterpiece in itself, featuring a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and local aesthetics. The elegant villas, each thoughtfully positioned for privacy, boast sweeping views of the pristine beach and verdant landscapes. With their thatched roofs, open-air living spaces, and modern amenities, the villas seamlessly merge indoor and outdoor living.
